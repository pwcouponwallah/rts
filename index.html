Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PW Real Test Series?

The PW Real Test Series is a comprehensive set of mock tests designed to simulate the actual JEE/NEET examination experience. It helps students evaluate their preparation level, identify strengths and weaknesses, and improve their test-taking strategies.

How do I use the 1000₹ discount coupon?

After registration, you'll receive coupon code via email. During checkout on the PW Real Test payment page, enter this code in the coupon field to apply your 1000₹ discount.

What is the validity of the coupon code?

The coupon code is valid for all tests in the 2025-26 series. You can use it for any test registration until March 31, 2026.

Where are the offline tests conducted?

Offline tests are conducted in over 200 cities across India. You can select your preferred exam center during registration. Detailed center addresses will be provided on your admit card.

What is the difference between online and offline tests?

Online tests can be taken from home with instant results, while offline tests are conducted in exam centers to simulate the actual exam environment with proper invigilation and protocols.

When will I receive my admit card?

For offline tests, admit cards are available for download 3-5 days before the exam date. You'll receive an email notification when your admit card is ready.

What kind of performance analysis is provided?

After each test, you'll receive a detailed analysis including:

  • All India Rank (AIR)
  • Subject-wise and chapter-wise performance
  • Time management analysis
  • Difficulty level breakdown
  • Comparative analysis with toppers
Can I get a refund if I miss a test?

While refunds aren't provided for missed tests, you can access the question paper, solutions, and analysis afterward for self-practice.
